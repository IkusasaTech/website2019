CERTIFICATIONS

It's one thing to hold a degree or point to acquired knowledge of IT concepts and theories, but it's quite another to be able to prove, in practice, your command of technology and leverage it to make business more efficient. That's why ICT certifications are so valuable to businesses. Industry certifications from various trusted vendors are meant to demonstrate that ICT professionals can apply the classroom knowledge in a real-world, on-the-job scenario

Some of the reasons to get certified :

• Many employers use IT certification as a requirement for certain jobs. • Many IT employers use certifications to differentiate between equally qualified candidates. • The ICT industry see IT certification as very valuable. • A good number of hiring managers consider certification as part of their hiring criteria • In high-growth industries, entry-level employees who hold an ICT industry Certification can command higher salaries than their peers.

Microsoft Training

Microsoft Training

Microsoft in-person and online IT training will give you the expertise you need in a specific product or technology. When you learn from our Microsoft Certified Trainers, you can be confident that what you’ll learn will be accurate, complete, and up to date, so that you can tailor your path towards Microsoft Certifications. The following certification options are available for various technologies linked to Servers, Desktops, Applications, Databases and Development Platforms :

• Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA). If you’re just getting started in technology, MTA might be the certification for you. This certification addresses a wide spectrum of fundamental technical concepts, and it assesses and validates core technical knowledge in three areas: IT infrastructure, databases, and software development.

• Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP). If you’re looking to advance your career in the tech world or to gain benefits, MCP certification is the gold standard. MCP certifies skills across a range of Microsoft technologies as Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (MCSA), Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert (MCSE), and Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer (MCSD) certifications to help you get a job, a promotion, and a better salary.
