IBM Overview

IBM aligns thousands of training solutions that align with its hardware, software, and services offerings. There are numerous Advisors that can help you with career path and certification planning to the selection of courses that fit your budget and meet your specific learning goals. Aligned with an IBM Global Training Provider, Ikusasa Technology Solutions will help you define your needs relative to your goals and set up private customized courses whenever required. We can therefore help you to :

• Plan your career path and help you stay on target

• Identify certification tracks and courses that meet your training objectives

• Select a specific training class

• Explain the benefits of various delivery methods

• Stay within your budget

• Obtain detailed quotes

There is tremendous opportunity for professionals in the IT industry. But it is also easy to be lost in the crowd. It is the credentials held by an IT Professional that can be the determining factor of recognition and success. IBM Professional Certification offers credentials for successful IT Professionals across the industry.

IBM Professional Certification is available in all areas of the business, including IBM Analytics, IBM Cloud, IBM Security, the IBM Watson technologies, and more. IBM has a complete range of certification, from entry-level to advanced, that include the many different job roles available for IBM Certified IT Professionals.
