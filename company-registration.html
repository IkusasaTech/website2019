Learning Management System

Ikusasa develop web application used to administer, document, track, report and deliver educational courses or training programs. Learning management systems are used by educational instructors to support classes that meet in a physical classroom or to facilitate fully or partially online courses. Our learning management system a built to accommodate the discipline of instructional design, which involves designing and developing learning experiences that leverage the technological advantages of an online environment.



PRIVATE COMPANY

    Private Company(PTY) LTD: R975.00
  • Incorporated by 1 or more persons for financial gain for the shareholders.
  • Must have a minimum of 1 directors.
  • Unlimit number of directors.
  • Restricted to offer securities to the public and is also restricted from transferring its shares.
  • Granted Proprietary Limited or "(Pty) Ltd” status.
  • No annual audits required if all requirements are met.



NONPROFIT COMPANY

• Incorporated by at least 3 persons for public benefit, social activities, communal or group Interest, etc. • Must have a minimum of 3 directors. • Income/property may not be distributed to the incorporators, members, directors or officers. • Only reasonable compensation for services rendered by them may be taken. • The name of a non-profit company will end with “NPC”.



PERSONAL LIABILITY COMPANY

• Minimum of 1 directors. • As a professional, you can register a company that you want to be personally liable for. • Owners are liable for any debts and liabilities incurred. • This type of company is registered by professionals such as Doctors, Lawyers, Engineers, Accountants, etc. • Registered personal liability companies are granted Incorporated (Inc) status.

BECOME BEE COMPLIANT

Any entity with an annual revenue less than R 10 million qualify as an Exempt Micro Enterprise (eme) and can apply for a Bee Eme Affidavit. If your annual sales (turnover) is more than 10 million you cannot apply for a Bee Affidavit and will have to contact a Sanas vertification agency for a Bee Qse Certificate of Generic Bee Certificate




VAT REGISTRATION

If the VAT application form is posted/dropped into SARS mailboxes at their branches, the registration process may take much longer.
• COMPULSORY REGISTRATION: Any business whose turnover exceeds or is likely to exceed R 1 million per annum.
• VOLUNTARILY REGISTRATION: Any business whose turnover is at least R 50,000 but less that R 1 million per annum.



SARS TAX CLEARANCE CERTIFICATE

Tax clearance certificate is a document issued by SARS and include the entity TAX, PAYE, VAT number, etc.
• The tax clearance confirm that you tax affairs are all in order with the SARS.
• The tax clearance certificate is valid for 1 year.
• The tax clearance certificate can be used for "tender" or "good standing".
